Our Story

From humble beginnings to global recognition

The Journey

SNM Esports was founded at the end of 2023 by Nick Kon with a vision to create something different in the competitive gaming landscape. What started as a small group of passionate players quickly evolved into something much bigger.

By mid-2024, SNM had made a name for itself in the esports community through consistent performances and innovative approaches to team management. The organization caught the attention of Max, who saw the potential for global expansion.

With Max's involvement, SNM Esports grew massively into a global esports organization, competing at the highest levels across multiple titles. Today, SNM represents excellence in competitive gaming with teams and players from around the world.
